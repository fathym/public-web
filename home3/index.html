Say goodbye to unwieldy frontend monoliths.

Fathym automates CI/CD builds, hosting and deployment so you can focus on building Jamstack sites or Single Page Applications with the JavaScript frameworks, Static Site Generators or no code builders you love. Steer clear of clunky frontend monoliths. Break your web project down into smaller and more manageable pieces for independent, flexible and scalable builds and deployments.
TAKE THE FATHYM JOURNEY

1. Start with your code.

Getting started is as simple as connecting your GitHub repository. Select an open-source recipe from your favorite frameworks or static site generators see Fathym in action or bring in your own code.

Your code.

Bring your sites and applications that you already have in GitHub or NPM.

Build and deploy.

Let us handle the automated build pipelines and deployments behind the scenes so you can stick to the good stuff.

2. Break it down.

Break website projects down into smaller apps with individual repositories and automated build pipelines for:
  1. Smaller, more cohesive, decoupled codebases.
  2. Smaller, faster builds.
  3. Simple testing, version control and release management.
  4. Incremental upgrades and independent deployments.
  5. Flexibility to fork and re-use modular apps for other projects.

You're a composer.

Build with the freedom to use different JavaScript frameworks, Static Site Generators or even no code builders on the one site. Use the right framework for the right job. Fathym effortlessly brings your project’s apps together to compose one cohesive and seamless website or web app on your custom domain.

3. Deploy with confidence.

The Fathym Global Edge Network leverages the Azure CDN to deliver your content and sites to the global points closest to your users. Take advantage of independent builds and deployments so you can work concurrently on your web project. Update to any version of your apps with version control, and easily rollback when things go awry without impacting your production site.

4. Scale as you grow.

Get started for free in a shared infrastructure environment built to scale with you and your customer's needs. Outgrown Fathym's shared infrastructure? Employ all the same great tools from your own managed or private Azure clouds.
Take advantage of our simplified developer experience while scaling to your own Azure cloud with full access to your own infrastructure and the full range and power of Azure's extensive services. Build a future-proof tech stack from the get go without the complexity.

Stay in sync with your team.

Collaborating and keeping updated with your teammate’s builds, pull requests and deploys has never been easier than through our interactive social feed. Fathym offers one consolidated workflow for you to view and manage your web projects and their corresponding repositories and builds.

Leverage the power of Azure minus the complexity.

Fathym leverages Azure for the most advanced, enterprise-grade infrastructure and world class performance, reliability and security. Control who accesses your apps through out-of-the-box authentication and identity management. As you scale and need the flexibility and full range of services that only the big cloud providers offer, we'll automate best practice infrastructure in your own Azure environment.

Why deploy with Fathym?

Fathym's powerful micro frontends allow work to be divided and conquered concurrently. Virtual developers handle the heavy lifting of the hosting and DevOps automation, saving you time and money. Plus, the freedom to use multiple frameworks, static site generators or no code builders on the one project! Take advantage of Fathym's streamlined PaaS developer experience while future proofing your tech stack in the knowledge that you can scale to your own automated Azure cloud when ready.
