Why deploy Docusaurus?

Build optimized websites quickly, focus on your content. Save time and focus on your project's documentation. Simply write docs and blog posts with Markdown/MDX and Docusaurus will publish a set of static HTML files ready to serve. You can even embed JSX components into your Markdown thanks to MDX. Extend or customize your project's layout by reusing React. Docusaurus can be extended while reusing the same header and footer.
