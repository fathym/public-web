Say goodbye to unwieldy frontend monoliths.

Fathym automates CI/CD builds, hosting and deployment so you can focus on building Jamstack sites or Single Page Applications with the JavaScript frameworks and Static Site Generators you love. Steer clear of clunky frontend monoliths. Break your web project down into smaller and more manageable pieces for independent, super flexible builds and deployments.
TAKE THE FATHYM JOURNEY

1. Start with your code

Getting started is as simple as connecting your GitHub repository. Select an open-source recipe from your favorite frameworks and Static Site Builders to see Fathym in action or bring in your own code.

Your code

Bring your sites and applications that you already have in GitHub or NPM.

Build and deploy

Let us handle the automated build pipelines and deployments behind the scenes so you can stick to the good stuff.

2. Break it down

Break website projects down into smaller apps with individual repositories and automated build pipelines for:
  1. smaller, more cohesive, decoupled codebases
  2. smaller, faster builds
  3. simple testing, version control, release management
  4. incremental upgrades and independent deployments

You're a composer

Build with the freedom to use different JavaScript frameworks or Static Site Generators on the one site. Use the right framework for the right job. Fathym effortlessly brings your project’s apps together to compose one cohesive website or web app on your custom domain.

3. Deploy with confidence

The Fathym Global Edge Network delivers your content and sites at the global points closest to your users. Take advantage of independent builds and deployments of your apps so you can work concurrently on your web project. Update to any version of your apps through our version control, and easily rollback when things go awry without impacting your live site.

4. Scale as you grow

Get started for free in a shared infrastructure environment built to scale with you and your customer's needs.
Outgrown Fathym's shared infrastructure? Employ all the same great tools from your own private or managed clouds.

Stay in sync with your team

Collaborating and keeping updated with your teammate’s builds, pull requests and deploys has never been easier than through our interactive social feed. Fathym provides one consolidated workflow for you to view and manage your web projects and their corresponding repositories.

Leverage the power of Azure minus the complexity

Fathym is built on Azure for enterprise grade infrastructure and security. Bring in your own custom domains, secure with SSL encryption. Control who accesses your apps through out of the box authentication and identity management filters.

Why deploy with Fathym?

Fathym's powerful micro frontends allow work to be divided and conquered concurrently. Virtual developers handle the heavy lifting of the hosting and DevOps automation, saving both time and money. Plus, the freedom to use multiple frameworks and Static Site Generators on the one project! Discover new ways to maximize resources and knowledge from your team with your code and low code and no code tools. Harness solutions from the open source universe or developed by your team.
